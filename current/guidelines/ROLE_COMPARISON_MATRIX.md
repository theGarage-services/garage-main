## 🎯 KEY DIFFERENCES

### **REGULAR RECRUITER** = Doer
- **Creates** jobs (needs approval)
- **Searches** candidates
- **Contacts** candidates
- **Schedules** interviews
- **Tracks** progress
- **Updates** status

### **MANAGER** = Approver & Team Leader
- **Approves** jobs
- **Creates** recruiter accounts
- **Assigns** jobs
- **Monitors** team
- **Reviews** shortlists
- **Handles** escalations

### **LEAD** = Strategist
- **Views** all teams
- **Analyzes** performance
- **Sets** quotas
- **Reviews** metrics
- **Adjusts** structure
- **Makes** strategic decisions

### **HIRING MANAGER** = Decision Maker
- **Reviews** candidates
- **Shortlists** or rejects
- **Schedules** interviews
- **Provides** feedback
- **Makes** hiring decisions
- **ONLY** for assigned positions

## 🔄 WORKFLOW EXAMPLE

**Hiring Flow Across Roles:**

1. **Regular Recruiter** creates job posting → "Submit for Approval"
2. **Manager** receives notification → Reviews → Approves
3. **Manager** assigns job to recruiter
4. **Regular Recruiter** searches candidates → Adds to pipeline
5. **Regular Recruiter** schedules initial interviews
6. **Hiring Manager** reviews shortlisted candidates
7. **Hiring Manager** schedules final interviews
8. **Hiring Manager** makes hiring decision
9. **Lead** sees analytics: time-to-hire, conversion rates
10. **Lead** adjusts team quotas based on performance

**Each role sees ONLY their part of the workflow!**

## 🎉 SYSTEM DESIGN PRINCIPLES

✅ **Separation of Concerns:** Each role has distinct, non-overlapping responsibilities
✅ **Least Privilege:** Users only see what they need for their role
✅ **Clear Visual Hierarchy:** Color-coded banners, role badges, and stats
✅ **Clean UI:** Features removed (not disabled) if not permitted
✅ **Consistent Framework:** All roles use same beautiful Solo Recruiter UI
✅ **Role-Appropriate Language:** Button labels match role capabilities
✅ **Smart Routing:** Role-specific actions map to appropriate views
✅ **No Confusion:** Clear info banners explain exact permissions

---

**This matrix ensures complete role isolation with zero feature overlap or confusion!** ✨
